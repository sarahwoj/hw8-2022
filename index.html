About the Artist

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter. Her discography spans multiple genres and her narrative songwriting—often inspired by her personal life—has received critical praise and widespread media coverage.

What's Taylor Up To?

On October 21st, 2022, Taylor released her 10th studio album, Midnights. The album was inspired by sleepless nights scattered throughout Taylor's life. Not only is Midnights her return to autobiographical pop, but it has been praised by reviewers and audiences.

  • Midnights is now the most streamed album of 2022
  • Midnights and its lead single, "Anti-Hero", were Spotify's most-streamed album and song in one day with 185 million and 17.4 million plays, respectively
  • Midnights debuted atop the Billboard 200 with 1.57 million units and marked Swift's fifth album to open with over a million copies sold
  • Swift became the first artist to occupy the Hot 100's entire top 10, with "Anti-Hero" at the top, "Lavender Haze" at number two and "Snow on the Beach" (featuring Lana Del Rey) at number four

Career Highlights

  • Has sold over 200 million records, making her one of the best-selling musicians of all time
  • The only act with five albums that opened with over one million copies sold in the US
  • Featured on rankings such as Rolling Stone's 100 Greatest Songwriters of All Time, Billboard's Greatest of All Time Artists, the Time 100, and Forbes Celebrity 100
  • Taylor has won:
  • 11 Grammy Awards, including 3 Album of the Year wins
  • 34 American Music Awards
  • 29 Billboard Music Awards
  • 84 Guinness World Records
  • 1 Emmy Award
